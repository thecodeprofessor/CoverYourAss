# Cover Your Ass

![Cover Your Ass Logo](cya_logo.png | width=100)

Introducing **Cover Your Ass**, a C# .NET MAUI mobile app designed to help you keep track of your daily activities, both professional and personal. This app is your steadfast companion, much like a trusty donkey, enabling you to record information, jot down notes, and capture pictures of various activities you undertake throughout the day.

**Cover Your Ass** provides you with a platform to record proof of the work you do. It ensures you have a record that you did it, and did it correctly. When it comes to providing proof, this app has your ass covered!

If you ever find yourself in a "pics or it didn't happen" face-off, don't sweat it! **Cover Your Ass** is your pocket-sized, truth-revealing sidekick, always ready to trot in with the evidence you need.

With **Cover Your Ass**, you can:
- Record proof of your activities.
- Take pictures to provide visual evidence.
- Keep your personal and professional evidence organized.

**Cover Your Ass** is more than just an activity tracker – it is your assurance and peace of mind in the palm of your hand. With this app, you can confidently go about your day knowing you have a reliable record of your activities at your fingertips. So, why wait? Start covering your ass today!
